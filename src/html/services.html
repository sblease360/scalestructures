Our Services

Full Building Model

We will work with you to bring each and every part of your structure to life, building each floor independently and connecting them magnetically to create a complete model with the option to view each floor individually.

Your finished piece will give you both a full exterior impression of your project and a thorough understanding of the internal spaces, helping you to visualise living or working in your finished building in a way that simple plans, and even on screen walkthroughs simply can't replicate.

Specific Room Model

We will work from your plans or other reference material to bring your planned room to life, complete with door and window openings and any cupboards, cabinets or appliances created in perfect scale with your room.

Trying to decide between several designs?

Let us bring all the options to life in scale before taking the plunge on your final choice.

Something Else

Need something that doesn't really fit into either category above, maybe you are looking for a model showing your whole street, or need a piece of scale furniture or other household object.

We can still help!

Our processes are very adaptable, and most objects can be 3d printed with the right design and processing.

Indicative Pricing

Although each project is unique, and each one is quoted individually once the scope of the job is fully understood, the table below will give you some idea of how cost effective a Scale Structures model can be.

Project Complexity

  • Simple - Single rooms and many single storey structures
  • Moderate - More challenging single storey structures and most two storey structures
  • Complex - Larger projects, more challenging designs and multi-storey structures

Project Size

  • Small - Up to 20cm x 20cm
  • Medium - Up to 45cm x 45cm
  • Large - Up to 80cm x 80cm

Moderate £250-£350 £350-£450 £500-£750
Complex £500+ £650+ £800+
